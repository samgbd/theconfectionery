About

Hi, I'm Shanna!

The Confectionery was born not only from my love of all things sweet, but from the love to share the joy I feel when I bake.

I like to play with flavors and continuously try new recipes, to which my husband and children play the role my Guinee pigs. I have yet to hear a complaint about that.

My focus is to take dessert up a notch. Elegant flavors in whimsical treats, made with a ton of love and happiness.

I had originally planned on starting this business a couple years down the road, when my youngest son goes off to middle school. However, the universe had another plan.

A friend of a friend was getting married and wanted a desert table. My lovely friend mentioned that I bake. The soon-to-be bride and groom took a chance on me, having never tasted my offerings. It was a beautiful success and not only did the event bump up my start date, but I also gained two lovely additions to my circle of friends.
